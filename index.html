Servr

Goal

We made Servr - desktop edition to address an issue. That issue? There are no simple web servers for the masses. Until now. We wanted to make a web server that any person with minimal technical knowledge could use without writing even one line of code. That is what guided us through the process of creating our Servr products.

Usage

Servr - desktop edition

  1. Follow the instructions in README.md
  2. Consult the wiki
  3. Port forward with your router (try portforward.com for some good tutorials based on your router model)
  4. Buy a domain from a registrar (e.g. register.com, or hover.com)
  5. Register your domain with a DNS provider (a great free one is afraid.org)
  6. Follow your DNS provider's instructions with nameservers (and anything else they tell you to do)
